Good Games with good Competitive Scenes

Valorant

This realatively new game, having only been released June of 2020, is one of the most popular games right now. Valorant is a FPS game, and it is like a mix between CS:GO and Overwatch. It is a 5v5 fps shooter where there are attackers trying to plant the bomb, and if the bomb blows up the attackers win, but if the defenders defuse the bomb or take out all the attackers before they plant the bomb the defenders win. Even though this game is very new it already has a very established competitive scene with dozens of teams and millions of fans supporting the teams. There have been many tournaments, but the best one has yet to come, it is called Valorant Champions.

Osu!

This simple but addicting game has been around since 2007. Osu! is a rythm game, where you click circles on the screen to the beat of the music. Though unexpected, this game does have a competitive scene, where players form teams and they compete with each other to win tournaments. Although many of the tournaments are on a small scale, there is one big tournament every year called the Osu! World Cup.

League of Legends

This tilting, but sometimes fun game has one of the oldest and most established competitive scene out there. League is undoubtedly one of the most popular games, but for those who don't know league is a 5v5 MOBA where you try to attack the enemies' base and break their nexus allowing them to win the game. League has had a competitive scene for 10 years and there are many seasonal tournaments in many different regions of the world. The biggest of those tournaments is the World Championship. This tournament comes around every year and many teams strive to win this tournament making watching these games very fun.
